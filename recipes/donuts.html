Donuts

Recipe and picture by @blancanutri


Ingredients

  • 1 small banana + 1 apple (it also works with carrot or pumpkin)
  • 35 gr oat flakes or oat flour
  • 2 eggs
  • Cinammon Ceylan
  • 1 tsp Coconut Oil
  • 2-4 ounces chcolate 85%
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Nuts (optional)

Steps

  1. Peel the apple (or carrot) and cut it in small pieces. Put it on a plate and microwave 2 min. without covering (if using carrot, heat it unti soft)
  2. Mix all the ingredients (smashed banana, apple, oat flakes, eggs, cinammon and baking powder) with a hand blender
  3. Put the mix in the donuts mold and bake 200 degrees 10-20 min.(pre-heated oven)
  4. Put the chocolate in a bowl with the coconut oil and warm it 1 minute and a half till it is in liquid form.
  5. Once the donuts have cooled down, cover them with the chocolate and you can sprinkle some crashed nuts
  6. It can be stored 1-2 days in the fridge
