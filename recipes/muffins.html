Almond Muffins

Recipe and picture by @blancanutri


Description

Easy mufffins without egg. Perfect if you want to have a quick and healthy snack. You can store them in the fridge for 2-4 days

Ingredients (for 6 muffins)

  • 1/2 cup (approximately 50gr) almond flour
  • 1/2 cup (approximately 35-37 gr.) wheat flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 (approximately 60 ml) glass milk
  • 1 tsp a sweetener of your choice
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp oil (10 ml)

Steps

  1. Pre heat oven 180 degrees
  2. Mix all ingredients till you have a compact dough
  3. Fill the muffin cup 1/4 of its capacity. Add cacao cream or half ounce chocolate (85%)
  4. We fill in the muffin cup till 3/4 of the capacity
  5. Bake 25-30 minutes 180 degrees
